01What it does

A writeup goes in exactly as the writer produced it. The formatter's one job is to return a complete, publish-ready Substack package — structured, styled and metadata-complete — without removing a word, reordering a paragraph, condensing an idea, or adding any opinion of its own. The scholarly, warm, unmistakably Nigerian voice of the publication is preserved exactly, never smoothed out or Americanised.

02What gets added

House style

Title Case bold titles, bold sentence-case section headings, bold italic subheadings — no decorative hyphens or hyphen bullet lists anywhere.

Pull quote

The single most powerful sentence in the post, rendered as a Substack blockquote at its most natural point.

Standard header & footer

A fixed author block and closing footer with links to the portfolio, the free STEM courses, and every social channel — inserted the same way, every time.

Button placements

Subscribe, share, external link and resource buttons, each tagged with its label, URL and exact placement instruction.

03Full delivery package

  • The formatted post — byline, standard header, body, pull quote, standard footer.
  • Image placements — where each image belongs, with a full generation prompt written for every one.
  • Button placements — using a fixed tag format naming the button type, label, URL and where it goes.
  • Post metadata — everything needed to publish with nothing left to fill in by hand.
