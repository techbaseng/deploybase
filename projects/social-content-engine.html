Social Content Engine

A single command produces a full day of on-brand social content for two organisations — researched live, written to a fixed voice, and compiled into a ready-to-post document, every time.

01What it does

Given a date, or the word TODAY, the engine locks that date as the single reference point for the whole session, then researches what's actually happening around it before writing a single word — public holidays, awareness days, and what's trending in Nigerian education and Nigerian tech over the previous 48 hours. Nothing is written from a stock content calendar; every theme is sourced live at the moment of generation.

02Two brands, two voices

Techbase Consultant Services

Forward-looking and youth-empowering, anchored in Nigerian STEM education. Every post must connect back to coding, robotics, AI literacy, web development or IT training for Nigerian youth and schools — never a generic tech-lifestyle post.

KBI Schools

Warm, encouraging and community-driven — the tone of a trusted educator speaking directly to a school family, covering school life, parenting, and the Nigerian school calendar.

03How a session runs

  • Resolve the date. Lock the exact day and date, confirm it explicitly, and use it as the anchor for both brands.
  • Research both themes live. A public holiday or major observance always overrides a general topic; every global headline is refracted through each brand's mission before it becomes a post.
  • Preview both themes before any content is written, so the day's direction is confirmed up front.
  • Write platform content for each company in its own fixed voice and tone.
  • Compile the formatted document for each brand, ready to hand off.
