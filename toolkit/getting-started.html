Getting started

Get Claude running in about 15 minutes

These three steps are the same no matter which tool you plan to use. Do them once, then head to the Exam & Revision Generator or Lesson Note Generator page to finish setting up that specific tool.

A Project, inside Claude, is a private space where you save an instruction once and upload your materials once. Claude remembers both permanently, so every time you come back you only need to type a short request. You never re-paste or re-upload anything.

1

Download Claude and create your account

Claude is free to sign up for and free to use for this purpose.

Look for the app published by Anthropic (white background, orange logo). Once installed, open it and tap Sign Up:

  • Fastest: tap "Continue with Google," choose your Google account, and you're in.
  • By email: enter your email, create a password, then open the verification email Claude sends you and click the link inside.
2

Create a Project

Create one Project for each tool you plan to use, for example, one named "Exam & Revision Generator" and another named "Lesson Note Generator." Each Project can hold materials for as many subjects and classes as you teach, so you don't need a new Project per subject.

On the phone app
  1. Open the Claude app.
  2. Tap the menu icon (☰) or find the Projects section.
  3. Tap New Project.
  4. Give it a clear name (e.g. "Exam & Revision Generator").
  5. Tap Create.
On the computer (claude.ai)
  1. Look for Projects in the left-hand sidebar.
  2. Click New Project.
  3. Name it after the tool (or your subject and class).
  4. Click Create.
3

Add the instruction and your materials

This is the only part that's specific to each tool, the exact instruction text and the files you upload are different for the exam tool versus the lesson note tool. Open the relevant guide below to finish setup:

Tool 1

Exam, Marking Guide & Revision File Generator →

Paste the exam instruction, edit your school name and address, upload your term e-notes.

 Tool 2

Weekly Lesson Note Generator →

Paste the lesson note instruction, edit your school name/state/location, upload your e-notes or topic lists.

Important: always copy the instruction text from this website (or the document your admin gave you), never type it from memory. The exact wording is what makes the formatting, marks and structure come out correctly every time.

Please do not share your Claude login with anyone, treat it like your email password.
